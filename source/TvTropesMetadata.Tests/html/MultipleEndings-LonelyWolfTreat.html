Warning! Due to the sheer amount of spoilers for these endings, they are all unmarked.

Main Series

  • While the original Lonely Wolf Treat games had a sole ending, in the complete series, alternative secret endings were added for certain chapters:
    • Chapter 1: Instead of going to Danny's to buy instant noodles, Treat doesn't put on her coat and stares through the shop's door. After a few minutes, Danny comes out and accuses Treat of acting creepily, with Juju joining in and convincing Danny to lock the store until Treat leaves. Left with no other choice, Treat goes out into the wood to scavenge for food, only to run into another pack of wolves, led by one named Timber. Initially skeptical because she recognized him as part of her old hunter pack, Treat soon joins them for a meal, eventually becoming a permanent member. After some time passes, Treat (now in her wolf form as it is customary for the pack) finds her first scavenge, a dead rabbit (heavily implied to be Boreas), which Timber congratulates her for. Later that night, Treat wants to go back to her cabin to get her coat because she's feeling cold, and Timber accompanies her against her wishes, revealing that his father was killed by rabbits in the process, which is why he wants to watch her back. While passing by, Juju happens to see the pair snooping around and becomes suspicious, so she confronts Treat at the cabin while Timber hides. Just as Juju is about to push Treat off the cliff, Timber comes out and pushes her instead, then gaslights Treat into thinking she did it. The two then head down to get the corpse, while Mochi appears to find Juju's scarf on the cliff side, leading her down to see Juju dead, breaking down crying and running awa. As Treat and Timber look over Juju's dead body, Treat realizes that Timber is the one who's been killing rabbits and leaving the corpses for the pack to find. The game abruptly ends here.
    • Chapter 3: While looking for props for her murder mystery play, Moxie stumbles across a safe in the back area, entering a code and opening it up. To her shock, she finds a gold bar and calls her friends to see. Moxie wants to sell the gold to get a lot of money, with Chai supporting the idea. However, Salt and Pepper don't agree due to possible legal trouble they could get into with Moxie's mom, as it could be her gold bar. Ultimately, Moxie and Chai leave to put their plan in motion, going to Ginseng on Chai's insistence to see their brother who works in the black market. Unfortunately, it'll take time to sell due to the gold being a huge asset, so Moxie decides to ask her mother for an extension. Currently, this is where it ends.
    • Chapter 6: Instead of living to go back to the hot springs, Mochi decides to find the culprit behind the garden alone, by any means necessary. She first goes to the Warren and encounters the Junior Guard and his brother again. Mochi soon gets into an argument with them that culminates in beating them up. Entering the Warren, Mochi notices something odd: the head of a wolf has been stuffed and hung up on the wall, nicknamed the Big Bad Wolf. After beating up the receptionist and stealing the rooms' keys, Mochi goes down in the basement to see more wolves who were killed and stuffed years ago, breaking her down further. A flashback is shown, revealing the Big Bad Wolf to have been Timber's father who was murdered by rabbits. During this time, Treat is worried sick due to Mochi's absence, so Annie reassures her and leaves to find Mochi, who by now broke into the tenants' room and beat them up, including the ones who wrecked the garden. Annie is talking to Mousse about the situation, only to be interrupted by Junior Guard claiming that Mochi attacked him and his brother. Annie and Mousse are dismissive, so Junior Guard decides to get Senbei instead, while Annie goes to talk to Danny. This results in Danny, Annie, Senbei and Juju to encounter Mochi just as she steps out of the Warren, having completed her revenge. Mochi also realizes the vandals wrecked her garden on Senbei's orders. When their attempts to calm Mochi down don't work, Senbei invites Mochi to fight him instead. During the fight, Senbei reveals his interest in Frosting due to its dark history involving wolves and rabbits and them killing each other. The fight ends with Senbei delivering a single critical blow that knocks Mochi down with injuries, much to the horror of Juju, Annie and Danny. The game then ends.

Spinoffs

  • DATE TREAT has multiple endings depending on your choices:
    • DATE TREAT:
      • Normal Ending: You leave the cabin on good terms with Treat.
      • Bad Ending: You hate the curry that Treat prepared for you. Treat gets upset and urges you to leave.
      • Mochi Ending: You reveal yourself to be Mochi, who was sent to the cabin by Moxie to prank Treat.
      • Moxie Ending: You reveal yourself to be Moxie, who came to the cabin in disguise to prank Treat.
      • Toffee Ending: You reveal yourself to be Toffee, who was sent to the cabin by Syrup to prank Treat.
    • DATE MOXIE:
      • Treat Ending: Treat admits that she was trying to prank Moxie, but doesn't like lying to her friends.
      • Trick Ending: Treat is revealed to be Trick, who was sent by the real Treat to prank Moxie.
      • Yuri Ending: Treat and Moxie go inside the tent and kiss a lot.
      • Netherworld Ending: Treat accidentally drags herself and Moxie on an adventure through the Netherworld because of a prank that got out of hand.
      • Poly Ending: Treat admits that she wants her, Mochi, and Moxie to all be together, and invites Moxie to sleep over.
    • DATE TRICK:
      • Traveler Ending: You decide to join Trick on their travels, giving you a lot more time to get to know each other.
      • Good Ending: You part ways with Trick, who promises to visit you again sometime.
      • True Ending: You become furious, because DATE TRICK is the only chapter with actual dating involved, and now it's over. Trick gets confused and decides to take their leave.
    • DATE MOCHI:
      • Revenge Ending: Mochi murders you as punishment for destroying her garden.
      • Darkest Ending: Mochi tortures you to death, then goes on to murder everyone else in Frosting.
      • April Fools Ending: Mochi reveals that she captured you as a prank and was never planning on actually murdering you.
      • Secret Sexy Ending: Mochi brought Treat and Moxie to the hot springs, and the three proceed to do definitely more than just kissing.

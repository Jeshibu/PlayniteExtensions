This page summarizes the many, potential endings of The Hundred Line -Last Defense Academy-. As a page about endings, all spoilers will be unmarked.

    open/close all folders 

First Playthrough

    Route 0 

  • Ending 0: It All Starts Now: Go through the motions of everything that happens without player input. The player is guaranteed to see this ending, which is not considered one of the 100 endings advertised, on their first playthrough. Eito suggests going along with the invaders' demands to get home. Nozomi begs the group that they need to stay and fight, only for them to turn against her with the sudden reveal that they know of her secret, creating a rift between her and Takumi in the process, Nozomi believing he had spread it against her wishes. On the day of the agreement, the negotiations turn out to be a trap by the Supreme Commander, who kills Nozomi when she runs to assist everyone stuck in the "escape pod" provided. Takumi kills the Supreme Commander and absorbs her cryptoglobin, giving him the power to finally understand the boy made of Undying Flames, who is revealed to be the power source of Last Defense Academy, the source of the Flames, and the battery for the Doomsday Device behind the locked doors. Eito then shows his true colors and misanthropy by destroying the generator, leading to a fight to the death and Takumi absorbing his cryptoglobin as the entire academy shuts down. Before he merges with Takumi entirely by proxy, the Ghost Boy shows himself one last time to tell Takumi that the merging of him, Eito, and Takumi's hemoanima has enhanced his Specialist ability to the point that he would be able to return to the first day of his time at Last Defense Academy. On the Hundredth Day, it's revealed the Academy had an escape pod prepped for the group all along, and Takumi decides not to board so that he can go back in time and Set Right What Once Went Wrong. Shortly after, the escape pod returns to Earth with nothing but a lone walkie-talkie, with Tsubasa and Takemaru trying to warn Takumi that something horrible and wrong is happening up in the Artificial Satellite.

Spare Eito Routes

    Last Defense Academy 2nd Scenario Route 

Ending Path (click to expand) Spare Eito, Reveal your time-travel secret, Save Second-Last Defense Academy, Reveal Nozomi’s secret, Sacrifice Kyoshika, Take Eva prisoner, Let Hiruko attempt to destroy Nigou, Save Gaku, Let the Undying Flames Boy join the team, Spare Eito again, Let Shouma kill Eva, Keep fighting for humanity, Allow Eito to rejoin the team, Defy Sirei one last time

  • Ending 001: Farewell, Last Defense Academy: The "true" ending to the extent there is one, as due to the laws of Time Travel in this setting all endings happen in The Multiverse (Word of God explicitly describes its name as a Pun; rather than being a Golden Ending, it is an ending where you learn a lot of the truth). The students discover most of their lives are faked, they are actually Super-Soldier Futurans grown to serve as humanity's champions with the exception of Nozomi, but decide to side with them because of Nozomi's kindness and realizing humanity is out of options, seeking to at least save Futurans in genetic vaults once they have their new home. V'ehxness, having cannibalized most of her fellow Commanders, learns to manipulate the Undying Flames and creates a missile she uses to destroy the Artificial Satellite, leaving Nozomi the sole human alive — but is killed before she can take over the planet as the "new God". Sirei, never having been programmed with instructions on what to do if humanity is destroyed, proceeds to activate the Doomsday Device and the group is forced to Mercy Kill Shion to stop the destruction of the Futurans, killing everyone except Nozomi. She finds a party of Futuran children who she makes peace with, implying that humanity's memory at least will live on.

  • Ending 002: Welcome to Last Defense Academy: This ending follows the "Last Defense Academy 2nd Scenario" Route until the 100th day where Sirei orders the students to evacuate the Academy so they won't get killed by the omni-directional attack. Instead of rebelling against him, Takumi and the students obey his last order. Sirei reconfigures the escape pod to return to the planet after all Futurans are killed so that the students can repopulate the planet. After the Futurans are wiped out by the Undying Flames, the students' memories of their adventure are removed on their return to the Academy, their backstories are replaced with happier alternatives, and Sirei instructs them to rebuild civilization as their new mission for humanity's future.

    Goodbye Eito Route

  • Ending 003: Praying For Your Happiness: Instead of accepting Eito back into the team after he blinded himself, Takumi rejects bringing him back, and he leaves the Academy to seek refuge in the Second-to-Last Defense Academy while grappling with guilt and possible forgiveness. When he arrives there, he discovers that Sirei packaged an Undying Flame Bomb that he was told to use if V'ehxness came for him, though he speculates if he was actually supposed to use it to kill himself. Soon, he meets a group of Futuran children refugees who decided to take refuge in the Academy, and he quickly relates to their struggles of not being forgiven as they disobeyed orders not to go outside and strayed away from their own group. Unfortunately, the Invaders catch on to their hiding spot and attack the Academy. Eito tells the children to run and that forgiveness is based on actions after mistakes instead of feelings of guilt, and as they flee he transforms and fights the Invaders in a Last Stand. When he's inevitably overwhelmed, he detonates the bomb, reveals that he asked Sirei to implant Fake Memories of him bonding and having fun with the other students and prays for the others happiness and victory in his final moments. The ultimate fate of the SDU is unrevealed.

Takumi chooses to save Kamyuhn, and while this leads to a war between humanity and the Futurans, Takumi and Kamyuhn manage to rebuild and unite the Futuran society, and eventually negotiate peace with Humans and end the war 15 years later. Although they have succeeded in creating peace on Futurum, Takumi still feels guilty over Nozomi's death and can only hope that she's happy with the result.

    Cult of Takumi Route 

Ending Path (click to expand) Spare Eito, Reveal your time-travel secret, Save Second-Last Defense Academy, Reveal Nozomi’s secret, Sacrifice Takemaru, Believe Nozomi (Ending 042), Believe Eito (Ending 041)

After sacrificing Takemaru, Takumi ingests some pills made by Yugamu that boosts his charisma and pheromones to be a better team leader and to improve his self-esteem. This results in all the other students falling madly in love with him, setting up a "reward" system as an excuse to be uncomfortably intimate with him and eventually setting up a cult centered around him. Nozomi, who isn't affected as much due to her weak hemoanima, questions if the current state of affairs will really be for the better and asks Takumi if he wants to be cured of his new condition, but Eito retorts that Takumi's newfound charisma is needed to unify the students under him and that they want him to be their ruler.
  • Ending 041: Overlord: Agreeing with Eito will make the other students brainwash Nozomi to love Takumi as well, and Takumi quickly rationalizes his newfound authority as necessary. V'ehxness arrives early on Day 54, where she declares her love for Takumi and desire to take him for herself instead of ruling over Futurum and winning the war. After killing V’ehxness, and Nozomi's "reward" time where he rapes her, Takumi fully embraces his role as the students' ruler and aims to take over both humanity and the Futurans with his brainwashed harem cult at his side.
  • Ending 042: Living Idol: Instead of agreeing with Eito, Takumi agrees with Nozomi to be cured of his condition and give everyone's free will back. The next day, Yugamu tricks Takumi into swallowing pills that are supposedly the cure, but they actually completely paralyze his body, leaving him unable to do anything but breathe and blink. The students declare their intention to keep him alive and immobile as they turn him into their "god", and beat Nozomi to death for trying to "take away their god". Soon after, Eito rapes Takumi while professing his undying love for him, and Takumi mentally shuts down, resigning himself to his fate as a living symbol.
